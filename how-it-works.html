Executive Coaching Roadmap

  1. Stakeholder Identification

    Identify and align with key stakeholders (supervisors, peers, sponsors) to set expectations and support the coaching journey.

  2. Assessment of Development Areas

    Conduct 360° feedback, personality instruments and performance reviews to prioritise strengths and growth areas.

  3. Formulation of Action Plans

    Create SMART goals, new routines and accountability structures. Map resources and support.

  4. Communications Plan

    Design transparent communication cadences, progress reporting and feedback loops to keep stakeholders informed.

  5. Establishment of Success Metrics

    Define baseline and target metrics, dashboards and ROI measures that align personal growth with business goals.

  6. Leadership Goals & Milestones

    Maintain momentum through tailored goals, milestone tracking and regular reviews. Summarise growth and provide recommendations.
